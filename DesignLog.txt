==Power Module and Serial Module
Power and Serial Module:
ICs & Logic
1x Zilog SIO/2 (Z84C4006PEC / Z84C4010PEC) [DIP-40]
2x CH340C USB-to-UART [SOP-16]
1x 74HCT688
2x USB-C Receptacles (HRO TYPE-C-31-M-12)
2x 20-pin Stacking Headers (2.54mm, dual-row, long pins)
1x SS-12D00 Slide Switch

Power & Protection
1x 3A Polyfuse Resettable Fuse
1x 47µF–100µF Electrolytic Capacitor (10V+)
4x 0.1µF (104) Bypass Capacitors

Resistors & LEDs
4x 5.1kΩ Resistors (USB-C CC lines Signal)
5x 1kΩ Resistors for LED
5x LEDs (1x Power, 2x Port A RX/TX, 2x Port B RX/TX)1x 6 DIP Switch
1x  6 Resistor Network




==CPU and Clock Module (8MHz)
ICs & Logic
1x Zilog Z80 CPU (Z84C0008PEC) [DIP-40] (8MHz rated CMOS version)
1x 7.3728 MHz Active Crystal Oscillator [DIP-14 / 4-pin]
1x 74HCT04 Hex Inverter [DIP-14] (Essential for clock buffering)
2x 20-pin Stacking Headers (2.54mm, dual-row, long pins)
Power & Protection
1x 10µF Electrolytic Capacitor (10V+) [Radial] (For Power-On Reset delay)
3x 0.1µF (104) Bypass Capacitors [Ceramic] (One each for CPU, Oscillator, and 74HCT04)
Resistors & Buttons
1x 10kΩ Bussed Resistor Network [DIP-9 or DIP-10] (For pulling up Z80 control lines)
1x 10kΩ Resistor [Through-hole] (For the Reset RC circuit)
1x Tactile Push Button (6x6mm) [Through-hole] (Manual Reset switch)
